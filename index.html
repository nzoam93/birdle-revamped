Birdle

Welcome to Birdle — the bird-brained cousin of Wordle!

You can guess any valid English word, but the final answer will always be a bird. 🐦

A blurry bird clue is located below the keyboard. Each guess sharpens the image until the big reveal!
(On mobile, you may need to scroll down.)

Hard Mode — for true ornithologists and bird nerds only!
